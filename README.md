# 📊 BT IntelliStat (Beta)

**AI-Powered Statistical Research & Analysis Platform**

BT IntelliStat is a web-based data science tool designed for senior researchers and data scientists to perform complex statistical analysis with a single click. It combines the power of **Python's Data Science stack** (Pandas, Scikit-Learn) with **LLM-driven insights** (via Groq/Llama-3) to provide deep, professional interpretations of your data.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Multi-Format Support:** Seamlessly analyze CSV, Excel (`.xlsx`, `.xls`), and Word (`.docx`) files.
* **AI Auto-Detect:** Let the AI examine your data distribution and recommend the most mathematically precise methodology.
* **Professional Methodologies:** Built-in support for K-Means Clustering, Linear Regression, ANOVA, PCA, and more.
* **Cloud History:** Securely save and retrieve your analysis history directly from your **Google Drive appDataFolder**.
* **One-Click Copy:** Export professional-grade tables and insights directly to Microsoft Word with a high-contrast format.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Backend:** Python 3.10+ / Flask
* **AI Engine:** Groq (Llama-3.3-70b-versatile)
* **Auth:** Google OAuth 2.0 (OpenID Connect)
* **Visualization:** Matplotlib & Seaborn
* **Deployment:** Render (with Gunicorn)
